One of the most famous rock band in the whole world from Rome

Four guys, four different personality: Victoria De Angelis, the bassist,Ethan Torchio, the drummer, Thomas Raggi, the guitarist, and last, but not least, Damiano David, the frontman.
This is the story of four, young guys who, in the last two years have traveled the world thanks to their energy and their music. The four guys joined together finding the right feeling very quickly: so Måneskin are born.
The name, Måneskin, means "Moonshine" in danish, the original language of Vic's mom.
Måneskin started playing around in Italy starting from Rome: they immediately distingued themselves for their bohemien style.

The beginning of their international career

In 2017 the band competes in a talent show, X-Factor, with a new song, Chosen. They conquer the public for the first time: everybody loves them!
After that, Måneskin became more and more famous. So, they published their first album, Il ballo della vita, and their european tour registers sold out everywhere.
In 2021, the guys partecipate in Sanremo, the most important italian talent show didicated to music in a new look glam-rock. They win the contest with a hard rock track, Zitti e buoni and their careers become an international success.

Måneskin: a strong identity

Thanks to the modernity, freshness and determination, Måneskin are now one of the most famous rock-band in the world: they release freedom not caring about people's opinion and this is perfectly in line with rock DNA.
